and a decline in the working age population.Some 733.5 million Chinese people were employed in 2022, according to the country’s statistics bureau. That’s down
from 774.7 million in 2019.

plans to raise official retirement ages.The drop reflects factors such as higher youth unemployment due to the pandemic as well
